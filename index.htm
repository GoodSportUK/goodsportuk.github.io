Good Sport Brand Guidelines

Overview

The Good Sport brand is composed of the icon and the wordmark. Together these make the logo.

The wordmark is typeset in 'M54 Athletic', and is always 90% black (#1a1a1a)

Dos and Don'ts

Do

  1. Put space around the icon/logo to let it breathe

Don't

  1. Stretch or skew it

Working with the SVG

The brand was designed in Inkscape as an SVG file.

  • The linework should always be the same weight, at any size, to preserve the density of the icon
  • The words and plus '+' device should never have a border/stroke
  • Only the 3 soccer balls have a fill. They have a fill which matches their border colour. No other elements except for the 3 soccer balls should have a fill
